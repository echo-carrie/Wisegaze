关于慧眸智行

“慧眸智行"是一个实时的疲劳驾驶检测系统，用于检测驾驶员疲劳驾驶程度并在检测到驾驶员疲劳驾驶以后做出警报，并协助驾驶员采取相应缓解疲劳的措施，最大限度地预防由于疲劳驾驶而产生的交通事故。"智慧眸 "包括以下模块和技术：

  • 基于人脸信号与脑电信号的疲劳驾驶检测技术
  • GPS 模块，提供距离驾驶员最近的休息点位置
  • AI语言聊天陪伴助手
  • 警报模块，实时监控系统状态并在达到疲劳以后发出警报

我们的目标

WHAT WISEEYE IS DOING?

通过结合脑电信号和人脸信号的分析，准确判断驾驶员的疲劳程度，提前发现潜在的疲劳驾驶风险，在发现潜在风险后采用警报、AI语音聊天、导航到最近休息点的方法来缓解驾驶员疲劳，以保障道路交通安全。

FORESTS ARE THE ROOT OF ALL LIFE

Pakistan has 4.2 million ha of forest and planted trees, which equates to 4.8% of the total land area. The forestry sector of Pakistan is the main source for lumber, paper, fuelwood, latex, and medicines. Five hundred thousand workers are employed in forestry and forest-related industries such as logging, village carpentry, and the manufacture of timber components for the construction industry. Forests supply 32% of Pakistan’s total energy needs in the form of fuelwood. Forests also provide forage for one third of Pakistan’s 86 million head of livestock. Leather, wood, and other livestock products contribute US$400 million, or 9%, of total export earnings. In short, forests play an important role in providing shelter, as over 2 billion people rely on them. As a source of food, shelter, water, fuel, and security, there is a greater need to preserve our forests. According to the FAO, there were 32.0k people employed in Pakistan's forest sector in 2010, which is a major reason to think about the preservation of forests.

DEFORESTATION – A PRE-DECIDED DEVASTATION

Deforestation is a major environmental concern in the world. Deforestation includes the cutting down, burning, and destructing of forests. It is the first link in a chain of environmental degradation that includes erosion, climate change, air pollution, and the loss of hardwood and fuel wood. Forests are being cut down in Pakistan at a petrifying rate. The one of the major causes of climate change in Pakistan is deforestation. In Pakistan, the agriculture, forestry and land-use sectors are responsible for 22% of global greenhouse gas emissions, and deforestation is a major driver. In Pakistan, forest fires were responsible for 56% of tree cover loss between 2001 and 2021. Out of which 4.6% of tree cover loss occurred in areas where the dominant drivers of loss resulted in Deforestation.

PROTECT FOREST TO COMBAT CLIMATE CHANGE

Forests are a climate hero, buffering communities against extreme weather and absorbing and locking away planet-warming carbon dioxide. They are valuable carbon repositories and play an important role for the climate. When forests are destroyed, they release large quantities of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere, which further exacerbates the climate crisis. It takes decades to reforest areas ravaged by wildfires. From 2001 to 2021, Pakistan lost 9.75kha of tree cover, equivalent to a 1.0% decrease in tree cover since 2000, and 3.56Mt of CO₂ emissions. Currently, Pakistan is facing adverse and severe effects of climate crisis in the shape of extreme temperatures, severe drought and forest fires.
